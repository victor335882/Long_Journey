STORY

The journey is start from Aug 31, 2015. In the beginning, I worked as a cleaner with my friend in Brisene and traveled around. However, It's hard to live here because of the low income and hight consumptions.

For saveing money, I moved to a small town Moree and worked with Australians. Those colleagues are all kind and wise. Working with them make me understand how to be calm in a hard time.

When this job was completed, I started to backpack across Australia and southeast Asia. There are many kind people and beautiful scenery in this trip.
